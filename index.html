Welcome

Read Like
Ray

Ray Bradbury, writer of Fahrenheit 451, educated himself entirely at the library — one short story, one poem, and one essay every night for 1,000 nights.

This app is his method, made into a daily habit. Every day you'll get a small queue of handpicked writing drawn from the themes you care about. Rate what you read, track your streak, earn badges, and see what your friends are finishing.
