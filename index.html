Welcome

Read Like
Ray

Ray Bradbury educated himself entirely at the library — one short story, one poem, and one essay every night for 1,000 nights.

This app is his method, made into a daily habit. Every night you'll get a small queue of handpicked public-domain writing drawn from the themes you care about.

Rate what you read, track your streak, and see what your friends are finishing. It takes 15–30 minutes a night.
